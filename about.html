Hello, I am Dave Sose, a Port Harcourt based Song and Producer, with an amazing sound that thrills

David Onaburhekan, professionally known as Dave Sose is a Port Harcourt based, Nigerian singer/songwriter who has been making music since his college days in 2016. With a Vocal texture that matches that of the likes of Weeknd, Tay Iwar and Chris Brown. Dave Sose shows a wide range of singing and songwriting, hitting notes and writing lyrics that portrays a mark of courage in sharing very personal stories about himself
